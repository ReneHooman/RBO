Just like you, we have also gone through the loss of a loved one and therefore understand the emotions that form part of your loss and grieve. We are there to assist and help you during this time.
Whether we like it or not, we have to accept the fact that death is one STOP street that all of us won’t pass. What will I do when that happens in my life?
The realization, “My loved one has passed away, help!!!”
The shock, torment, anguish, becoming distressed, disbelief, being rebellious, all emotions which form part of your reactions when death occurs in your family.
There is no warning or preparation for the shock when a loved one passed away, even if death was expected. It is a reality that form part of the mourning and acceptance process to help the left behind family to go through this with the least trauma.
The many questions that seem to have no answers must be answered, all the people that must be notified, what is the first steps, what do I do now?
We at RBO FUNERALS have the capability, the necessary knowledge and skills to assist you emotionally and empathetically in this time.
We will give our best under these circumstances to fulfil all of your needs to do all the arrangements applicable to give the family the most dignified funeral / cremation service for their your loved one.

What to do?


The First step

To take care of your beloved with one phone to RBO FUNERALS. We will assist you with the best advice, whether cause of death was natural or unnatural. We also have the necessary facilities to take care for your beloved one until the funeral / cremation service. 


Second step

Our arrangement consultant at RBO Funerals will contact you for an appointment at the comfort of your own home or at our office for the arrangement of the funeral / cremation. Our fully trained personnel will assist you in every aspect concerning the execution of the service. The church, the parson, organist, florist, catering, cemetery / crematorium and all relevant associates shall be contacted to affirm and verify all necessities required.


The Third step

We will provide all original and certified copy’s of documentation. This documentation will be need for policy claims at institutions as well as financial institutions.
